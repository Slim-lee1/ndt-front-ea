Our Reach

Serving a Broad Range of Industries

NDT East Africa Limited works with clients across the public and private sectors — from individual property buyers seeking peace of mind to large financial institutions verifying structural integrity for investment purposes. Our non-destructive testing methods are trusted because they are accurate, non-invasive, and professionally delivered.

Our Best Motivators

Our Target Clients

We partner with a wide range of stakeholders who rely on structural integrity data to make informed decisions.

Residential & Commercial
Private Developers & Buyers

Private developers and buyers who wish to verify the structural integrity of their construction projects before or during occupation.

Construction
Contractors

Contractors who wish to utilise non-destructive tests in their quality control and assurance for concrete works, and to allay any fears on the quality of their completed structures.

Professional Services
Project Consultants

Project consultants who demand NDT with a view to ascertain the structural integrity of a given concrete structure for their clients.

NGOs
Non-Governmental Organisations

NGOs that need to establish or verify structural integrity of their concrete structures may utilise NDT to support their operational and compliance requirements.

Finance & Insurance
Financial Institutions

Financial institutions that need to establish structural integrity of a structure for insurance or financial support purposes may propose NDT on such structures.

Government & Public Sector
Public Institutions

Public institutions that need to establish or verify the structural integrity of their concrete structures can rely on our certified NDT engineers for accurate, independent assessments.
