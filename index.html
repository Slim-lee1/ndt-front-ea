Our Story

NDT-EA At a Glance

At NDT East Africa Limited, we offer Structural Audit solutions on Concrete and Steel Structures. We also lease out a range of NDT equipment. Based in Nairobi, Kenya, our experienced team is committed to delivering excellent and satisfactory services.

Our key team is comprised of Professional Engineers and Technicians with more than fifteen years of relevant experience in the construction industry.

Visual inspection of Concrete Structures
Mapping of reinforcement details — location, diameter & spacing
Determination of relative strength and uniformity of concrete
Thermographic inspection of buildings and structures
Geotechnical Investigations on Foundation Works
Design Checks & Corrective works for structurally unsound structures

Our Services

Our Expertise

Precision-Driven Solutions

Our NDT solutions are built to meet the toughest industrial standards, ensuring precision and dependability where it matters most.

01
NDTs on Concrete Structures
Non-destructive testing to evaluate concrete strength, durability, and internal integrity without harming the structure. Includes Schmidt Hammer, UPV, and carbonation testing.
Get a Quote
02
NDTs on Steel Structures
Accurate testing methods to detect surface and internal defects, measure thickness, and ensure the reliability of steel elements across all load-bearing components.
Get a Quote
03
Foundation / Geotechnical Investigations
Subsurface investigations to assess soil conditions and foundation suitability, supporting safe and stable construction from the ground up.
Get a Quote
04
Crane Proof Load Tests & Inspections
Certified load testing and inspections to ensure cranes and lifting systems meet safety and performance standards. Full compliance documentation provided.
Get a Quote
05
Structural Audit Services
Comprehensive structural audits for concrete and steel structures. We provide detailed assessment reports, corrective design recommendations, and monitoring of structural cracks and deterioration. Call us: +254 721 414 663
